Alkis Kalavasis

Hi! I am a 4th year PhD student at the Computer Science Department of the National Technical University of Athens (NTUA) working with Dimitris Fotakis and Christos Tzamos. I completed my undergraduate studies at the School of Electrical and Computer Engineering Department of the NTUA, where I was advised by Dimitris Fotakis.

Research interests: Theoretical Machine Learning, High-Dimensional Statistics, Optimization, Computational Complexity
